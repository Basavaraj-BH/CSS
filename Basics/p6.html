Defining Color Value

Color in CSS most often specific in the following formates:

  1. A COLOR keybord - like - "red" , "green" , "blue" , "transparent" , etc.
  2. A HEX Value - like "#ff0000" , ''#00ff00" , etc
  3. An RGB values (RED, GREEN, BLUE)-like "rgb(255, 0, 0)
